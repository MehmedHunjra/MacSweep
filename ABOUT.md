# ABOUT MacSweep

## Overview
MacSweep is a powerful and intuitive Mac cleaning tool designed to enhance the performance and longevity of your Mac devices. It seamlessly removes unwanted files, cache, and duplicates, ensuring your system runs smoothly and efficiently.

- **System Optimization**: Boosts the performance of your Mac by freeing up valuable disk space.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design for easy navigation and operation, making it accessible for all users.
- **Comprehensive Scanning**: Thoroughly scans your system for unnecessary files and duplicates.
- **Scheduled Cleanups**: Allows users to set up automatic cleaning schedules for ongoing maintenance.
